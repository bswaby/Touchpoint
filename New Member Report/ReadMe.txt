Report that is sent out to our staff on Monday's that shows number of members along with new members the last 7 days.

Top of the report is just basic summary info for membership

Bottom of the report are the new members in the last 7 days along with any additional involvements they are involved with

Setup
To get started, copy the contents of New Member Report.py to a new python script.  Doesn't matter what you call it.  Script will run naturally without edit.
